Garden Plants

Be creative with what you grow!

Sunflower

Sunflowers are a great addition to many gardens because of their bright yellow coloring and recognizable shape.

Daisies

They are a classic addition to many gardens.

Golden Pothos

The golden pothos is a sturdy plant, and is known for improving air quality.

Rose

Roses are another timeless flower for any garden.

Fittonias

Fittonias are an interesting plant due to the many colors that their veins can be.

Mentioned throughouth this page are examples of only a small fraction of all the plantlife that can be incorporated in gardens.

Gardens provide lots of opportunity for creativity, especially with the plants that can be used!
